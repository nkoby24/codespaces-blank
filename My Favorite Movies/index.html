My Favorite Movies

Fight Club

Fight Club is a movie directed by David Fincher. It stars Brad Pitt and Edward Norton.
This is a hard movie to explain, but it is by far my favorite movie. If you want a fun, action
packed movie, you will love Fight Club. Watch the trailer here.

Whiplash

Whiplash is an excellent movie directed by Damien Chazelle. It stars Miles Teller and
J.K. Simmons. It tells the story of a young drummer who is seeking out becoming one of the
greats. His abusive teacher pushes him past his limits every day in the hope of making him great. This movie is a great watch.
Watch the trailer here.

Interstellar

Interstellar is a great movie directed by Christopher Nolan. It stars Matthew McConaughey
and Anne Hathaway. Interstellar is a great movie about space travel. If you are into that you will love this movie.
Watch the trailer here.

Superbad

Superbad is a hilarious movie directed by Greg Mottola. It has a stacked cast with Jonah
Hill, Michael Cera, Seth Rogen, Emma Stone, and many more. If you are looking for a movie
that will keep you laughing, you will love this.
