Oreo Milkshake

This recipe will give you the best Oreo Milkshake. If you are a fan of Oreos and milkshakes, this recipe will be perfect for you.

This recipe will take around 5 minutes and will make 2 milkshakes. Each milkshake has 693 calories, 33g of fat, 90g of carbs, and 12g of protein.

Ingredients Needed

  • 1 Pint of Vanilla Icecream
  • 1 Cup of Milk
  • 8 Oreos
  • 1 Tablespoon of Chocolate Sauce
  • 2 Oreos(Garnish)

Steps to Make

  1. Place the vanilla ice cream, milk, 8 Oreos, and chocolate sauce into the blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Crush the remaining 2 Oreos in a bag. Look for a crumbly consistency.
  3. Pour your milkshake into 2 glasses and top with your crushed Oreos. Enjoy your Oreo Milkshake!
